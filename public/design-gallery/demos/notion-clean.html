🎯 Overview

This document outlines our product strategy for the first half of 2026. We're focusing on three major initiatives that will drive growth and improve the user experience across all platforms.

💡
Key Insight: User research from Q4 2025 shows that 73% of users want better collaboration features. This should guide our priority decisions.

The roadmap is organized by team, with cross-functional dependencies clearly marked. Each initiative has been reviewed by leadership and approved for the allocated budget.

"The best products are built at the intersection of what users need and what technology makes possible."
— Product vision statement, Jan 2026

📋 Q1 Initiatives

▶ Real-time Collaboration Engine

Building a new real-time sync engine using CRDTs to support concurrent editing by up to 50 users simultaneously. This replaces our current OT-based system.

  • CRDT implementation for text and structured data
  • Presence indicators and cursor tracking
  • Conflict resolution UI for complex merges
  • Performance target: <50ms sync latency
⚠️
Dependency on the Infrastructure team's WebSocket migration (due Feb 15).
▶ AI-Powered Search & Discovery

Semantic search across all workspace content, with natural language queries and smart suggestions. Uses our fine-tuned embedding model.

▶ Mobile App Redesign

Complete redesign of the mobile experience focusing on quick capture, offline support, and gesture-based navigation.
